What is Nuwa?

Whitepaper

Nuwa is not just a platform; it's a new paradigm for social interaction. Built on the Solana blockchain, Nuwa functions as the Real-World Social (RWS) Layer, bridging the gap between digital identity and real-world experiences. We believe that technology should enhance, not replace, human connection. Through AI-driven tools, community incentives, and a focus on authenticity, Nuwa is building a world where your digital presence enriches your real-life social fabric.

How it Works

Real-World Social (RWS) Layer

Nuwa memperkenalkan RWS Layer, sebuah infrastruktur yang memungkinkan data sosial dunia nyata terintegrasi dengan blockchain. Hal ini menciptakan identitas digital yang lebih autentik dan terverifikasi.

Sistem Tokenisasi

Interaksi sosial Anda, seperti menghadiri acara atau bertemu dengan komunitas, akan diverifikasi dan diubah menjadi aset digital yang dapat dipertukarkan, memberi Anda kepemilikan sejati atas aktivitas sosial Anda.

AI-Powered Social Discovery

Didukung oleh AI, Nuwa menganalisis data terverifikasi Anda untuk memberikan rekomendasi yang dipersonalisasi. Ini membantu Anda menemukan acara, komunitas, dan koneksi baru yang benar-benar relevan dengan minat Anda.

Roadmap

Phase 1 — Foundation (Q3–Q4 2025)

  • Launch Nuwa Human Search Engine (Beta): photo and name search (July 2025).
  • Introduce Nuwa Stones (points) → early gamified contribution rewards (July 2025).
  • Begin profile creation & enrichment by community contributors (July 2025).
  • Achieved 2000+ paying users and 5M+ views on TikTok and Rednote (August 2025).
  • TGE + Initial $NUWA token distribution: airdrop, public sale, liquidity pool.

Phase 2 — Expansion (Q1-Q2 2026)

  • Launch AI Identity Layer → verification tags distinguishing humans vs AI entities.
  • Launch plugins and mobile app for seamless photo-based search.
  • Introduce Nuwa World API → enable 3rd-party integrations (dating apps, HR tools, KYC services).
  • Grow a global contributor community through ambassador & UGC programs.

Phase 3 — Ecosystem Growth (Q3-Q4 2026 and beyond)

  • Launch Nuwa Network → social features built on verified identity profiles.
  • Introduce Nuwa DAO → community-driven governance for data policies, rewards, and ecosystem growth.
  • Enable AI-ready knowledge graphs to power next-gen agents, assistants, and research tools.
  • Long-term vision: become the infrastructure of RWS (Real World Social) and the unified human knowledge layer of the internet, where identities are permanent, transparent, and universally accessible.
